📋 How to Use

  1. Drag the "🔄 RTL Toggle" button above to your browser's bookmarks bar
  2. Navigate to any webpage where you want to toggle text direction
  3. Click the bookmarklet in your bookmarks bar
  4. Watch as the page switches between LTR and RTL direction!

✨ Features

  • Toggles between Left-to-Right (LTR) and Right-to-Left (RTL) text direction
  • Shows a visual notification when toggled
  • Remembers your preference using localStorage
  • Works on any webpage
  • No installation or permissions required
  • Privacy-focused - no data collection
